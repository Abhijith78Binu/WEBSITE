Frequently asked questions?

1

What Is the Dress Code for the Golf Course and Clubhouse?

Our golf course and clubhouse dress code is intended to allow for comfort and style while upholding a standard of civility and respect for the game of golf. Our goal is to make sure that everyone who visits the club has a good time in a setting that honors the history and essence of the game.

On the Golf course

Men: You must wear fitted shorts or golf pants with collared shirts. Sandals, sports shorts, jeans, and t-shirts are not allowed.

Ladies: Sleeved or sleeveless blouses with collars are appropriate golf attire. It's suggested to wear shorts, slacks, or golf skirts. Flip-flops, sportswear, and denim are prohibited.

2

What Are the Club's Operating Hours?

Normally, the golf course is open during the day. The opening and closing hours may vary based on the season and amount of daylight. Tee times can be reserved by golfers during these hours of operation.

3

Is There a Handicap System in Place?

A thorough handicap system is used at our golf club, which is necessary to foster a level playing field among players with different ability levels. In order to ensure that handicaps accurately reflect each golfer's potential ability, this approach computes handicaps based on players' most recent scores as well as the relative difficulty of the courses played. Members may easily track and enter new scores with our mobile app and website, which both provide frequently updated handicaps. This technique helps with personal skill improvement in addition to leveling the playing field and improving game enjoyment and equity. It is an essential instrument that embodies our dedication to fairness and the integrity of the sport, suitable for recreational play as well as fulfilling tournament participation requirements.
